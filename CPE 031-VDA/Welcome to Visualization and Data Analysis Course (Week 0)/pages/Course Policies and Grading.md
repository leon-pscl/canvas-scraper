Course Policies

On Attendance: Students are required to adhere to the University Policy on attendance. Failure to do so shall merit the prescribed course of action stipulated in the student handbook.

On Submission of Outputs: It is expected that students submit any form of outputs such as seat work, laboratory exercises, homework, projects, activities according to the prescribed deadline. Late submissions shall yield a deduction to the final grade of the submitted output.

On Cheating: As an institution that develops competent, responsible and God-fearing individual, TIP does not tolerate any form of cheating. Any students caught cheating for the first time shall receive a zero mark on a graded work. Second offense would mean an automatic withdrawal from the class.

Learning Management System and Communicating to the Professor: The following software platforms shall serve as platform for digital interaction between the students and the professor:

TIP Canvas: This shall serve as our official learning management system. Class materials such as power points, videos, workbooks are posted will be posted in this platform. Additionally, class announcements shall be posted by the Professor using this platform, where students can also ask questions or provide comments, whichever is necessary.Likewise, students are expected to maximize this when doing digital submissions such as laboratory experiments, seatwork, or final project.
