Important to Remember:

1.  Please be prepare and be online 10 minutes before the scheduled time for the conference.

2.  Make sure all your devices are all set up. Test your microphone and earphones.

3.  Choose your good location to have a clean and non-distracting background.

4.  Mute yourself if you experience unavoidable background noise.

5. Be at your decent and presentable dress while the online class is on-going.

6. Behave accordingly and give your full, focused attention. Apologize for any accidental breech of etiquette.
7. For the question or clarification about the topic, you may open your webcam or type your concern on the chatbox. Questions will be answered after the discussion.
8.  Attendance is a must. In case you cannot attend the conference, please inform a day before.

A conference recording copy will be posted here for your reference.

9. Check your canvas and your email regularly.

For concerns regarding the conference schedule, please let me know.
