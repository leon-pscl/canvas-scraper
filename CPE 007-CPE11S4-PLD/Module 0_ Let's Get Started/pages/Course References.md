ProQuest Academic Library

This combination of general reference volume and scope makes it one of the broadest, most inclusive general reference databases ProQuest has to offer. Subject areas include Arts,  Business,  Education, General interest, Health & Medical, Humanities, Law, Military, Multicultural, Psychology, Sciences, Social science, Women’s interest, etc.
