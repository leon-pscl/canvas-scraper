Intended Learning Outcomes
At the end of this module, The students should be able to:

Distinguish between user- visible and control/status registers, and discuss the purposes of registers in each category.

Discuss the implication of a RISC architecture for pipeline design and performance.

Compare and contrast techniques of improving pipeline performance in RISC machines and superscalar machines.
