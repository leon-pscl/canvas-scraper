Technology Requirements for Simulation Activities
The technology that will be needed for this course is only your computer (Desktop or Laptop or Mobile Phone) then an internet connection. Our main online resources for testing and simulation will be:

Tinkercad (For Breadboarding and Arduino circuit simulation) which you can access here
Tinkercad - [https://www.tinkercad.com/](https://www.tinkercad.com/),

Circuitverse for simulating logic circuits which you can access here
CircuitVerse - [https://circuitverse.org/](https://circuitverse.org/)

Circuit Diagram for making circuit schematics along with your Simulation here Circuit Diagram - [https://www.circuit-diagram.org/editor/#](https://www.circuit-diagram.org/editor/#)

Another alternative for Circuit Digram is Schematics - [https://www.schematics.com](https://www.schematics.com).
Note: You can choose which software/web platform to make your circuit schematics.

To get started on how to use these tools, I have provided learning guides from the platform creators of Tinkercad and Circuitverse themselves:

Tinkercad's Official Blog - [Basic Arduino LED Experiments using Tinkercad Circuits](https://blog.tinkercad.com/2018/04/19/basic-arduino-led-experiments-in-tinkercad-circuits)

Tinkercad's Official Tutorial on Arduino - [Learn Arduino](https://www.tinkercad.com/learn/circuits)

CircuitVerse Official Getting Started Guide - [CircuitVerse Getting Started](https://circuitverse.org/gettingStarted)
